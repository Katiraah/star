WELCOME NOTE FROM THE GOVERNOR

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you all to the Bomet County Government. I extend my heartfelt appreciation to the people of Bomet County for placing their trust in me by electing me as your Governor. I am committed to serving with humility, integrity, and dedication.My administration is determined to formulate sound economic policies, enhance revenue mobilization, and ensure the efficient allocation and prudent utilization of resources to promote sustainable economic growth and development across the entire county. We will adopt a program-based planning and budgeting approach aligned with our County Integrated Development Plan (CIDP) to holistically address service delivery challenges.We are committed to implementing transformative policies that will position Bomet County as a model of excellence in Kenya. Our focus will be on strengthening revenue collection strategies, expanding productive infrastructure, creating employment opportunities for our youth, and fostering wealth creation within our communities.I call upon our stakeholders and development partners to support county government programs as we work together to build an integrated, inclusive, and self-sustaining economy.
